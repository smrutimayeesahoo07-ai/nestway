About This PG

Sunrise Boys PG is a well-maintained, fully-furnished accommodation located in the heart of Patia, one of Bhubaneswar's most student-friendly neighborhoods. Just 10 minutes from KIIT University and 15 minutes from BPUT, it offers a safe, comfortable and focused environment for students.

The PG offers both single and double sharing rooms, all equipped with beds, study tables, wardrobes, and high-speed WiFi. Three nutritious meals are served daily by an in-house cook. Regular housekeeping and 24×7 security ensure a hassle-free stay.
